Offense

You will learn how to throw like an elite quarterback, run routes and catch like an elite wide receiver, and run hard like an elite running back. This part of camp will be led by the Carolina Panthers' QB, Cam Newton

Defense

You will learn the techniques of how to become a good defensive player, including how to sack quarterbacks, tackle running backs, and intercepting the football from wide receivers. This part of camp will be led by the Seattle Seahawks' CB, Richard Sherman

Special Teams

You will learn the proper ways of kicking and punting the football with great accuracy and power. You will also learn strategies of how to return punts and kicks. This part of camp will be led by the Baltimore Ravens' Kicker, Justin Tucker

Mission and Vision

Here in Football Camp hosted by Jayson Walla, we are committed to teach people, young and old, to learn the game of football better. Whether he or she had any experience or knowledge about football, we are committed to make our participants better, with the help of the most highly experienced coaches and trainers, including the help of three of the most accomplished NFL Superstars, Cam Newton, Richard Sherman, and Justin Tucker
